Who I Am

Hello! I'm Michael Murphy, a Job Captain at Bull Stockwell Allen based in San Francisco. I'm passionate about design that is rigorous, detailed, and grounded in the experience of space.

With a B.Arch from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo and experience managing project documentation and coordinating consultant teams, I enjoy working across all phases — from schematic design through construction administration.

My portfolio draws heavily from my student work, where I explored topics ranging from adaptive reuse and materiality to urban infill and housing typologies.
